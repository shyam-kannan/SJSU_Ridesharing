✅ Desired Behavior

One trip card → taps into DriverTripDetailsView with both riders

Root cause (likely)

The driver home screen is probably iterating over bookings (one per rider) rather than trips (one per posted trip). The fix is to deduplicate by trip_id and show aggregate passenger counts.

Also: tapping any trip in "Your Posted Rides" will navigate directly to DriverTripDetailsView — no separate view needed.
